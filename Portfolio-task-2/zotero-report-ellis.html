    Notes:

    • Black and white photo of Green Ponds store. Four men standing outside. H.W.Ellis. Established 1833.

      1. Exterior of H.W. Ellis’s General Store, Taken c. 1880.
      2. Staged
      3. Four men standing on the kerb outside the building. Woodens boxes stacked in a pile, and a hand-trolley outside the door. Cans can be seen stacked in the window, plus other displays of goods.
      4. Four men standing quite stiffle, hands by their sides, or in their pockets. Looking at the camera.
      5. Large tree can be seen behind the building. Road appears to be dirt. Lettering along the building above the windows, just under the roof it quite clear until the last window, when it is too faded to be clear.
      6. Eye is drawn to the windows, and the lettering just under the roof.
      7. Shows clothing and style of stores in the period.

      4. 10-20 people standing, or sitting on the horse/cart. No facial expressions can be discerned.
