Kempton Streetscape Study Summary
24 June 1997
History of "Streetscape Study"

24-4-97 * Southern Midlands Council called a public meeting of Kempton residents
to consider a proposal for a "streetscape study" of Kempton.
Meeting held in the Kempton Council Chambers, 7.30 start.
Present: ,

Mayor Colin Howlett, Deputy Mayor DJ Manning,
CLRS C Cartledge, P Tankard,
Greg Walker (General Manager),
Rob Vincent (Municipal Architect) and Peter Newton,
Paul Gilby (Gilby Roussos Architects) and Noel Vollus,
plus 17 local residents.
Paul Gilby engaged as facilitator.
* Southern Midlands Council agreed to invite Gilby Roussos Architects to
submit a “streetscape study" proposal.

30-4-97 : Gilby Roussos Architects submitted proposal.

6-5-97 * Southern Midlands Council formally engaged Gilby Roussos Architects.

18-5-97 *  All-day public workshop held at Kempton Community Hall.

23-6-97 * Presentation by Mayor Colin Howlett of awards to Kempton School
children who participated in the "streetscape study" colouring
competition.

. 24-6-97 * Public evening meeting to consider and discuss draft “streetscape study",
Kempton Community Hall.
8-7-97 ‘ Presentation of final "streetscape study" together with implementation
strategies by Gilby Roussos Architects, Kempton Municipal Hall.
